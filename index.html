Our Story

Four decades of
making houses
feel like home.

What started as a small family dream over forty years ago has grown into Woodbridge's most beloved home decor destination. We're Italian, we're loud, and we treat everyone who walks through our door like family — because to us, you are.

Every arrangement is handcrafted. Every display is curated with the same care we'd put into our own home. Come in for a vase, stay for the coffee.

The Shop
